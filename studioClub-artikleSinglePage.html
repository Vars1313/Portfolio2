‘Twas a Month before Christmas’ by Sandra Falconer

Sandra Falconer · 25 Dec 2021

Today we want to wish all of our members a very Merry Christmas! To celebrate, we are sharing a beautiful poem by member, Sandra Falconer, which opens with a few words about what inspired her to write this piece. Whatever you’re doing today, we hope you have a joyful Christmas and we look forward to seeing you at our events next week!

I wrote this poem, bit by bit, as last year’s festive season unfolded and completed it only a few weeks ago. Writing, and laughing, about something that’s not going as well as it might is my way of robbing a situation of its power to dampen my spirits. I hope that reading it has the same effect on you.

‘Twas the Night before Christmas’ was my starting point because the classic poem, ‘Twas the Night before Christmas’ has always been one of my favourites.

Merry Christmas!
